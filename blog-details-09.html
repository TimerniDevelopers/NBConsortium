8 Instagram Metrics You Should Be Tracking

Instagram analytics will give your employees a clear view of how you are performing overall when it comes to updating them during meetings. You'll be in the dark very easily without analytics. But what should you do? We have outlined eight KPIs in this guide that you should be monitoring in order to achieve growth.

Change your social media tactics for globalisation and to attract more customers with the help of a qualified professional. Remember one true fact you are losing every day many valuable prospects and sales because of your indecision.

01. Engagement

This indicator will demonstrate the degree to which your content is engaging your viewers. You'll also be able to tell whether it was successful in getting them to your website or business. You can calculate your engagement rate using a formula.

Total followers / Total engagement

Also, you'll discover that Instagram, like the majority of social media networks, offers stats for both your account and each post.

02. Comments received

You want your readers to "like" your piece as the first response. Getting them to leave a comment on the piece is the best response, but it can be difficult at times. Those that connect or relate to your material frequently do this. Prioritize looking after these users and keeping them interested. As they continue to leave comments, there is a very strong possibility that more people will see your article.

They might begin mentioning their friends in their comments as well. You gain the chance to gain new followers.

03. Follower growth

If you think about it carefully, growing your following indicates that you are doing great! You should congratulate yourself on successfully drawing customers to your account. While it is normal for sporadic spikes to occur, particularly if you run a paid advertisement or a significant campaign, your goal should be steady and moderate growth over a set length of time.

Seeing this kind of growth on your account shows that users are interested in what they see and want to learn more about what you have to offer. Also, a rising following count indicates the potential audience for your upcoming content. This will undoubtedly have an effect on your upcoming engagements.

04. Impressions

This metric will provide you with information on the precise number of individual Instagram users who saw your post. Your organic reach on Instagram will be aided by the platform's algorithm, which is directly correlated with your follower count and the quality of your content. And keep in mind that if your content is very engaging, Instagram may promote it to users who are not currently following you. You'll be helped in developing engaging content if you have an understanding of the types of material that typically draw the Instagram audience in.

05. Reach

It can be challenging to gain traction at first, but if you conduct an audit and acquire a sense of what your audience wants, it gets simpler to write and promote this content.

06. Referral traffic

In the end, you'd also like to direct your Instagram followers to your website. When your Instagram followers also go to your website, you have a good chance of increasing sales, attracting additional prospects, or just getting a return on your investment. But it presents a problem because the clickable link to your website can only be seen on your bio.

Another choice is to include URLs in your captions or create content that is so compelling that viewers will want to visit your website for further details. UTM parameters, which can record data from every user who copies and pastes your link into their browser or sends it to a friend, can be used to track referral traffic.

07. Conversions

The only thing that matters in the end is conversions. Do people answer the way you want them to when you ask them to do something? If not, there is something you need to fill in for in order to get them to react as you like.

Conversions can take many different forms, including your targets' purchasing your goods, making appointments, sending emails, or signing up. Consequently, a high conversion rate indicates that your material is very effective. High returns could potentially result from effective content.

08. Instagram Stories

Some users like to quickly glance through your Instagram Stories to check what has been posted today. Your writing should ideally maintain their interest long enough for them to want to learn more. They will either follow you on your primary account, peruse your postings, or visit your website. You might want to look at the Impressions, Story Exits, Story Responses, and Story Taps Back and Forward to see if your Instagram Stories were successful in drawing in viewers. These metrics can help you gain a better understanding of how your audience interacts with the content you deliver to them.
